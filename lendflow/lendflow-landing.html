How it Works
Simple for both sides
Whether you're lending or borrowing, WeLend makes the process transparent, fast, and secure.
01
Create Your Profile
Register with your NIN and verify your phone number. Lenders get licensed under WeLend's Bank of Uganda framework.
02
Post or Browse
Lenders post standing offers with their rate and terms. Borrowers browse offers or post their own funding request to the marketplace.
03
Match, Approve & Fund
Lenders review applicants and approve with one tap. Funds disburse via MTN MoMo or Airtel Money within 24 hours.

Why WeLend
Built for East Africa
Everything designed for how money moves in Uganda — MoMo, Airtel, local ID, local rates.
MoMo & Airtel Disbursement
Approved loans land in your MTN or Airtel wallet within 24 hours. No bank account required.
Bank of Uganda Licensed
All lending happens under WeLend's regulated umbrella licence. Borrowers and lenders are fully protected.
Live Loan Calculator

Dual Marketplace
