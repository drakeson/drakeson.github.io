How It Works
Simple on both sides
Whether you're a lender or borrower, WeLend is built around one principle: get to the money faster.
For Borrowers Get Funded
1
Create your profile
Sign up with your NIN and phone number. Verification takes under 5 minutes.
2
Browse offers or post a request
Search lender profiles by rate and type, or post your funding need and let lenders find you.
3
Submit your application
Upload your NIN, bank statement, and guarantors. Everything done on your phone.
4
Get funded in 24 hours
Once the lender approves, money lands in your MoMo or Airtel wallet within 24 hours.
For Lenders Start Earning
1
Register & get licensed
Complete NIN verification and get onboarded under WeLend's Bank of Uganda licensed umbrella.
2
Post your lending offer
Set your interest rate, loan range, and accepted types. Your profile goes live on the marketplace immediately.
3
Review applications
See each borrower's credit score, documents, and guarantors. Approve or decline with one tap.
4
Collect repayments & earn
Repayments are tracked automatically. Interest earnings hit your wallet on each instalment date.
