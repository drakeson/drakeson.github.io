How the marketplace works
I am a Lender
1
Register & get licensed under LendFlow's Bank of Uganda framework. Takes one day.
2
Post your lending offer — set your rate, amount range, accepted loan types, and duration. Goes live immediately.
3
Review applications from borrowers. See their credit score, documents, and guarantors. Approve in one tap.
4
Funds disburse, interest accrues. Track repayments from your portfolio dashboard.
I am a Borrower
1
Create your profile with your NIN and phone number. Verification takes under 5 minutes.
2
Browse lender offers publicly on the marketplace, or post your own request and let lenders come to you.
3
Apply or receive offers. Upload documents, add guarantors, and submit — all on your phone.
4
Get funded in 24 hours. Money lands in your MTN MoMo or Airtel wallet after approval.
