FAQs
What platforms do you develop for?

I specialize in native iOS development (Swift/SwiftUI) and Android development (Kotlin/Jetpack Compose).
I also build cross-platform solutions and web applications using React and Vue.js.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A simple mobile app takes 4-8 weeks,
while enterprise solutions may take 3-6 months. I provide detailed estimates after understanding your requirements.

What services do you offer?

I offer native iOS & Android development, UX/UI design, full-stack web development,
API integration, payment gateway implementation, and ongoing app maintenance and support.

Do you work with startups or enterprises?

I work with both! I have experience building MVPs for startups and scaling applications
for enterprises. My approach adapts to your business size, budget, and timeline.

How do you ensure app quality?

I follow industry best practices including comprehensive testing, code reviews,
CI/CD pipelines, and performance monitoring. Quality assurance is built into every stage of development.
